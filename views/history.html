A Vision in the Mist

Established as a premier boutique retreat in the heart of Ella, Morning Dew was born from a desire to harmonize luxury with the raw, untamed beauty of Sri Lanka's hill country.

Every stone, every balcony, and every view was carefully considered to ensure our guests feel connected to the landscape while enjoying world-class comfort.
