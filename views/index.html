A Sanctuary in the Heart of Ella

Morning Dew Hotel is a boutique hillside hotel located in Ella, Sri Lanka. The property is surrounded by lush mountains and offers panoramic views of Ella Rock and the beautiful hill country landscape.

Experience stylish rooms with balconies overlooking the mountains, modern amenities, and a relaxing atmosphere for travelers. Our hotel features a rooftop terrace, an infinity pool with mountain views, and world-class Sri Lankan and international cuisine.
